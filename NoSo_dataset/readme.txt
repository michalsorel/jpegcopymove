The 'NoSo_copymove_database' consist of original and forgered images with mask of the ground truth: 
- 13x original {01.png, 02.png, ..., 13.png}
- 18x forgered {01_mod.png, 02_mod.png, ..., 13_mod.png}
- 18x ground truth mask {01_mod_gt.png, 02_mod_gt.png, ..., 13_mod_gt.png}
